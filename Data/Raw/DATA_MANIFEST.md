# 📂 Raw Data Manifest & Access Instructions

Due to GitHub's file size limits and data privacy best practices, the raw microdata files are not hosted in this repository. 

To fully replicate this study, the following **11 datasets** must be acquired and placed in this exact folder (`Data/Raw/`):

### 1. Household Surveys (Encuestas de Hogares 2005-2015)
*   **Source:** National Institute of Statistics of Bolivia (INE).
*   **Access:** Publicly available at [INE Data Portal](enlace_al_ine).
*   **Files required:** `EH_2005.sav`, `EH_2008_limpia.dta`, `EH_2009.sav`, `EH_2011.sav`, `EH_2012.sav`, `EH_2013.sav`, `EH_2014.sav`, `EH_2015_limpia.dta` (original databases).

### 2. Employment Surveys (ECE 2016-2025)
*   **Source:** National Institute of Statistics of Bolivia (INE).
*   **Files required:** `ECE_4T15_3T25.dta` (original database).

### 3. Macroeconomic Series (Inflation and GDP)
*   **Source:** National Institute of Statistics of Bolivia (INE).
*   **Files required:**
    *   `ipc_departamental.csv` (Consolidated data, for real wage deflation)
    *   `pib_ine_bo.xlsx` (Consolidted data, for Output Gap calculation)
